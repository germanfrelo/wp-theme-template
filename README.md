# IfisEducación

## Overview

This repository contains the resources and documentation for the IfisEducación project, a specialized training platform in social intervention. We offer online and in-person courses for professionals such as social workers, social educators, and psychologists, among others.

## Project Goals

- Facilitate access to high-quality training in social intervention.
- Offer a wide variety of courses on relevant and current topics.
- Adapt to the needs of our students with flexible training formats.
- Provide customized training services for entities and companies.
- Ensure the quality of our training with recognized accreditations and endorsements.

## Key Features

- **Online courses:** on-demand and live.
- **In-person courses:** at our facilities or at our clients' facilities.
- **Customized training:** for entities and companies.
- **Comprehensive management of training plans:** for companies.
- **Bonus management:** for companies.
- **Video conferencing support.**
- **Blog:** with articles and resources for social intervention professionals.

## Technologies Used

- WordPress: For website content management, with a custom-built theme designed for block functionality and site editor compatibility.
- E-learning platform: For online course management.
- Video conferencing tools: For live courses and online support.
